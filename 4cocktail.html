Sex on the beach

Ingrediants:

  • 4 cls Wodka
  • 2 cls Pfirsichlikor
  • 8 cls Orangensaft
  • 1 cl Grenadine
  • 6cls Cranberry-Nektar
  • Ice (Crushed)
  • 1 Orangenscheibe
  • 1 Cocktailkirsche
  • 1 Zweig Minze
  • 6 Eiswurfel

how to prepare it:

  1. Place the ice cubes in a shaker with the vodka, peach liqueur, grenadine and cranberry juice, close and shake vigorously.
  2. Pour the crushed ice into a glass. Strain the drink through a bar strainer. Then top up with freshly squeezed orange juice.
  3. Garnish with a slice of orange, cocktail cherry and mint.
